One Gun Man: The Story

One Gun Man is a fast-paced First Person Shooter where you must eliminate members of the Hyper-Light Fanatics while collecting energy cores to charge your gun. Reach the end of the level to progress deeper into the Hyper Light Fanatics hideout, and take out their leader.

In the year 3030, the terrorist group which goes by the name of Hyper-Light Fanatics (HLF), has opposed the New Earth Union (NEU) for several decades. The Hyper-Light Fanatics stand against what the New Earth Union has become, calling them corrupt and unjust overlords of the common people. The Hyper-Light Fanatics have cost the New Earth Union billions in sabotage and trade route ambushes. The New Earth Unionâ€™s military has been working on a new weapon that could change the way battles are fought and set a new precedent for law and order. This weapon has the functionality of a complete arsenal as well as utility tools that give the user new ways of combat and infiltration. The military has selected a highly decorated combatant who has taken down countless Hyper-Light Fanatics and is sending them on a secret mission to take down the head of the Hyper-Light Fanatics organization. With this disruption in power, the New Earth Union hopes it can cause enough destabilization and cowardice in the Hyper-Light Fanatics ranks to finally get the foothold they need to wipe out the Hyper-Light Fanatics entirely.
